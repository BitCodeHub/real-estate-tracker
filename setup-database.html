Setup Database Connection

Step 1: Get Your Render Database URL

1. Go to your Render dashboard: https://dashboard.render.com

2. Click on your database: real-estate-tracker-db

3. Find the "Connections" section

4. Copy the External Database URL (it starts with postgresql://)

Example format:

postgresql://username:password@hostname.render.com/database_name
